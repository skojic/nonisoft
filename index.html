About Us

Nonisoft is a family web development agency which specializes in front and back end development. We are doing also system and network planning and administration for various company sizes. We are based in Belgrade, Serbia. Our main goal is quality work and satisfied clients.

Our satisfied customers

We participated in many projects for various satisfied clients. The biggest are: FJELLINJEN, Norwegian leading road toll provider. LOGMEIN, big chatbot and customer service provider based in Herzelia, Israel. SENOR, Serbian customer support agent company.

Planning

We believe that careful planning is important part of every development process. Design, mockup or any other branding solution is thoroughly discussed with client, trying to find a way, for online presence in a best possible way. Close collaboration between our client and us in this phase is essential.

Unique Design

Although we can offer some CMS solutions, we believe that unique design is best way to stand out from the rest of the web pages. We are trying to include all the colors, brands, logos, concepts in a unique way, trying to follow design trends and best practices. Our webpages are clean, neat and unique.

Development

After planning and design we believe that real fun is starting. Development phase is maybe the most important of all. Why so? We believe in scalable and easy maintanable products. Every CSS or SASS code we produce follows BEM methodology. Also we prefer clean, organized and maintenable code.

Client Satisfaction

Because Nonisoft is a family agency, we treat each our client like a family member. Your wishes and hapiness are important for us. You are important part of your product's growth. Constant communiation, small sprints, breakpoints is just one of the ways we are influencing your smile and satisfaction every step of the way.
