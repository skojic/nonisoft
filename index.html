About Us

Nonisoft is a family web development agency which specializes in front and back end development. We are doing also system and network planning and administration for various company sizes. We are based in Belgrade, Serbia. Our main goal is quality work and satisfied clients.

Our satisfied customers

We participated in many projects for various satisfied clients. The biggest are: FJELLINJEN, Norwegian leading road toll provider. LOGMEIN, big chatbot and customer service provider based in Herzelia, Israel. SENOR, Serbian customer support agent company.
